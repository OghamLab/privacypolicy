Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this app (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how data is used.

Information We Collect

Location Information: The app may request access to your device’s location to determine your city and load relevant local news headlines. This information is processed on your device and is not stored or shared.

How We Use Information

  • To determine your local region for news content
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. News content is retrieved from Google News RSS feeds, but no personal information is transmitted to Google.

Data Storage

We do not store personal data. All processing occurs on your device.

Permissions

The app may request location permission to provide local news. This permission is optional, and the app provides fallback content if denied.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
